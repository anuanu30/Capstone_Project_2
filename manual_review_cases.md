# Manual Review Cases

This section includes customer cases where the retention decision is not straightforward and requires manual business interpretation.

---

# Customer Case 1

## Observation
- High monetary spending
- High return rate
- Recent purchase activity declining

## Decision
Classified as High Return Customer

## Reasoning
The customer still contributes revenue but shows dissatisfaction risk through repeated returns.

---

# Customer Case 2

## Observation
- High frequency purchases
- Heavy discount dependency

## Decision
Classified as Discount Sensitive Loyal Customer

## Reasoning
Customer engagement is strong, but profitability may depend heavily on discounts.

---

# Customer Case 3

## Observation
- High spending
- Poor average rating

## Decision
Needs retention attention

## Reasoning
High-value customer with possible service dissatisfaction risk.

---

# Customer Case 4

## Observation
- Very recent customer
- High first-order value

## Decision
Classified as New Customer

## Reasoning
Insufficient purchase history to determine long-term behaviour.

---

# Customer Case 5

## Observation
- Previously frequent purchases
- Long inactivity period

## Decision
Classified as At Risk

## Reasoning
Customer historically showed strong engagement but now demonstrates churn behaviour.

---

# Customer Case 6

## Observation
- Moderate spending
- Extremely high delivery days

## Decision
Requires service recovery attention

## Reasoning
Poor delivery experience may negatively impact future retention.

# Customer Case 7
